Precise
Octave exceeds ANSI standards for a real-time analyzer. Each sub-band is calculated with a bandpass Butterworth filter, giving constant resolution across the entire spectrum range.
Speedy
All calculations are performed with Apple's proprietary Accelerate library, giving the fastest and most accurate filtering on the iPhone. All graphics are rendered with OpenGL, so you never miss a peak.
Convenient
You might need to bring out the big guns from time to time, but with Octave, you'll always have an RTA in your pocket, no matter what.
Intuitive
Single-tap to see the peak frequency.
Double-tap to pause.
Tap and hold to inspect sub-bands. It's that easy.
